Who We Are

Chess built for the Merrimack Valley.

Merrimack Valley Chess (MVC) is a student-run organization dedicated to growing competitive chess across Merrimack Valley high schools. We run team-based tournaments that give players a real competitive stage and connect schools across the region.

Whether your school has a seasoned team or is just getting started, we want to make it happen.
